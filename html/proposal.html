Mental Health Awareness App

Problem/Data Domain

  • Overview

    The idea of mental health has been a growing issue over the past 20 years. It has become more of a priority and resources for receiving help are needed now more than ever. Mental health can include stress, anxiety, poor feelings about one's self, and many other aspects of thought.

  • Information Technology Problem

    Information regarding a person's own mental health is hard to research. There are a multitude of issues going on with a person and researching by yourself can be tedious and straining. Most tests for mental health are deep within web pages of a psyciatric facility's page or need to be paid for to receive a diagnosis.

  • Previous Existing Solution

    Most existing solutions to mental health issues are a list of things that people can do. It does not diagnose the problem but just creates a general structure as to how a person would treat multiple different mental health issues. An idea of such list can be found here.

Web App Description

  • Users

    Anybody experiencing mental health issues who are seeking professional resources. This app will benefit minority groups, who might find that mental health resources in their communities are not very accessible, and even looked down upon.

  • Features

    We will have a quiz that the user takes when first signing up for the app. Finally, another feature is a meditation mode for users who are feeling anxiety and stress. The meditation mode will guide users through breathing exercises in order to calm them down. One last feature we will have is a chatbot that is integrated into our app. This chatbot will give users instantaneous support for less life threatening mental health issues.

  • Information

    Users will be able to see information about potential healthcare professionals in their area, who specialize in the type of care the user needs. Additionally, for users in need of quickly accessible emergency help resources, we will provide information about things like crisis hotlines and emergency medical services.

  • How App Solves Lack of Mental Health Resources

    It gives users a centralized location to find all the help and resources they might need to treat their problems. It also simplifies the process so that anyone can be quickly and easily directed to the resources they require.
